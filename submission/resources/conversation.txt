I interviewed the administrator of a small business that provides transport 
for school-going children in the Westlands zone. Hazina Transport Services 
targets parents with children at Shani School and Kianda School who reside 
in Westlands and need reliable transport for their children to and from school. 
The company offers two packages: 
1-way package for parents who only need either morning drop-offs or evening pick-ups
2-way package providing full, round-trip daily transport covering both commutes. 
The owner's preferred brand colors are blue, purple, and nude shades. 
Additionally, the final website must include professional photography and 
clear contact information.
